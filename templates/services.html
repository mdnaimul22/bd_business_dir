Precision Metal Components

Our facility is equipped with advanced milling and shaping machines to produce high-precision metal components. We ensure every component meets rigorous quality standards before leaving our workshop.

  • Industrial Flanges: Custom-bored and faced flanges for high-pressure piping systems.
  • Bearing Housings: Precision-machined housings ensuring perfect fit and alignment.
  • Gears & Sprockets: Custom gear cutting and tooth shaping for power transmission.
  • Bushings & Sleeves: Bronze, brass, and steel machined to exact tolerances.

Heavy Engineering

We maintain one of the largest lathe capacities in the region, capable of handling oversized industrial components. Specialized in heavy-duty lathe work for large scale industrial rollers and shafts.

  • Long Shaft Works: Capacity up to 12 feet (3657.6 mm) with 32-inch diameter.
  • Large Diameter Face Works: Turning components with outer diameter up to 1470 mm.
  • Industrial Rollers: Ideal for maritime shafts, textile rollers, and flywheels.
