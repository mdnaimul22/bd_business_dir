Earthmoving Repair & Maintenance Services

Bangladesh Engineering Workshop BEW™ understands how important it is to have your essential earthmoving equipment maintained and ready to support your projects, large or small. Our team includes earthmoving mechanics and specialist digger mechanics experienced in providing crucial earthmoving equipment repairs, maintenance and excavator breakdown services to keep your business-critical machinery up and running. What’s more, our earthmoving equipment repair services can come to you for optimum efficiency and affordability.

Our BEW™ earthmoving services:

  • Complete Engine Rebuilds: Diesel engine repairs and overhauls for excavators and dozers.
  • Bucket & Boom Repair: Welding, line-boring, and structural reinforcement for wear parts.
  • Track & Undercarriage: Repairing rollers, idlers, and track chains.
  • Hydraulic Systems: Pump/motor testing and cylinder resealing.
  • Mobile Repairs: On-site breakdown support to get you moving again.

Major Service & Overhauls

Over time, heavy construction machines need more than a regular service. Our major service includes detailed diagnostics, technical spec updates, and component upgrades. We service all major brands including CAT, Komatsu, and Hitachi.
