Truck Repair Services

Staying one step ahead of repairs is essential to keeping your fleet operational. We offer expert maintenance and repair services for all heavy trucks, ensuring safety, reliability, and longevity.

Our Truck Repair Services:

  • Regular Maintenance: Detailed inspections including tyres, engine oil, greasing, filter replacement, and diagnostic checks.
  • Major Service: Recommended every 30,000km, including brake/clutch fluid replacement, transmission oil changes, and full engine checks.
  • Emergency Repairs: Quick diagnosis and repair for breakdowns to get you back on the road.
  • Chassis & Axle Work: Structural repairs, chassis straightening, and axle alignment.
