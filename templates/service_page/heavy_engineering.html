Machine Capacity & Technical Specifications

We maintain one of the largest lathe capacities in the region, capable of handling oversized industrial components.

Long Shaft & Roller Works

For long workpieces up to 12 feet (3657.6 mm), we can accommodate a maximum outer diameter of 32 inches (812.8 mm). This is ideal for large industrial rollers, maritime shafts, and textile rollers.

Large Diameter Face Works

For shorter workpieces (length 450-500 mm), our faceplate capacity allows us to turn components with an outer diameter up to 1470 mm. This capability is perfect for large flanges, flywheels, and heavy gears.
